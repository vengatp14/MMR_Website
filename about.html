Building Excellence Since 1995

At MMR Electrical Hardware Pvt Ltd, we have been delivering trusted construction materials and hardware solutions for over two decades. Established in 1995, our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred wholesaler and partner for builders, contractors, and industries across Tamil Nadu.

From premium electrical hardware to essential construction materials, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of safety and durability. With a dedicated team and a strong supply network, we continue to support projects of all scales—residential, commercial, and industrial—helping our clients build with confidence.
